How do I send and receive funds?
To send funds from Jaxx, select the Send button. In the first box, enter your recipient's address, or select the QR icon in the text field to scan their QR code. In the second box, enter the amount of bitcoin / ether you want to send. If you wish to enter the amount in fiat currency, select the dollar symbol on the left. Select Send.

Jaxx offers two methods to receive funds: scan the sender's QR code with the camera function, or generate a QR code requesting a specific amount by entering the amount and selecting Generate.
How can I convert between coins / tokens?
As of Jaxx v. 1.0.2., Jaxx partnered with leading blockchain asset exchange, ShapeShift, to enable in-app conversion. Select the fox icon on any wallet screen to view your ShapeShift options. A toggle will appear with the available coins for that wallet (e.g., BTC -> ETH). Select whether you want to convert in crypto or fiat currency, enter your amount in the Amount field then hit Shift.
How do I backup Jaxx?
Jaxx offers users a 12-word Backup Phrase and a Pairing Code to backup and restore your funds, private keys, and transaction history. Go to Tools -> Backup Wallet to view and verify your Backup Phrase. We recommend writing down an exact copy of your Phrase and keeping it in a secure location.
How do I sync Jaxx across devices?
From the Pair Devices menu, select Display Pairing Code. This will display both the QR code and the Backup Phrase associated with your wallet. Use the other device to scan the QR code (mobile/tablet only) or enter the Backup Phrase manually.
How long will it take to receive funds after they've been sent?
It may take a few minutes to a few hours dependent on where your funds are being sent from. If your funds are being processed by an external service, such as an exchange, it may take up to an hour for you to receive your funds in Jaxx. It is also dependent on the level of activity on the blockchain. The bitcoin blockchain has been extremely congested so your bitcoin may take longer to appear, especially if you did not select a high mining fee.
Does it cost anything to send and receive funds?
Jaxx is completely free to download and use on all platforms. However, the blockchain networks that administer your funds will charge a small processing fee per transaction to ensure that the transaction goes smoothly and to reward miners. Jaxx itself takes no commision.
Can I point my mining rig payouts to Jaxx?
As Jaxx is a lightweight wallet that is not designed to receive mining payouts, we do not recommend pointing any payouts to Jaxx as it may cause issues on your end as well as potentially causing issues on our end. Click here for more information.
What's an HD wallet and why does it change my addresses?
HD stands for "Hierarchical Deterministic." HD wallets generate a new address every time funds are received to your current address. This helps to keep your funds private. All previous addresses can be reused to receive funds and add to your Jaxx balance.

In Jaxx, all coins are HD; however, all Ethereum ERC-20 tokens are not.
What happens if I lose my Backup Phrase?
It is vital that you write down and store your wallet's Backup Phrase in a safe location, as it is the only way you can restore your entire Jaxx wallet. Your Backup Phrase is stored locally on your device so if you lose your Phrase, we will not be able to help you.
Do I need internet access to use Jaxx?
In order to send funds and refresh your balance, you will need to be connected to the internet. However, you can take a screenshot of your QR code to enable users to send funds to your wallet whether or not you are online.
Which devices can I access Jaxx?
Jaxx is available for most phones and tablets. It is also available as a desktop application for PC, Mac, and Linux, as well as an extension for Chrome. You can pair your wallet across all devices.
Can I use unconfirmed funds?
Yes, you can use the funds in your Jaxx before they are displayed as Confirmed.
What does Transfer Paper Wallet do?
Transfer Paper Wallet sends the funds from an existing private key into Jaxx by scanning its QR code or entering it in text form. This will not overwrite your existing wallet.
How do I add a security PIN?
In Settings, select Setup Security PIN. Enter a 4-digit PIN then re-enter to confirm. This PIN will now be prompted to be entered whenever the Send function is used and whenever the wallet's pairing tools and private keys are accessed. This is basic security and we recommend utilizing your device's security features (e.g., fingerprint, lock screen, etc) for more advanced security.
How do I reset my wallet?
The procedure to reset your wallet varies between devices. See more information here.
My question isn't here! I need more support.
Head over to our support page on jaxx.io to let us know about your specific problem with as much information as possible! Our Customer Success Team will get back to you as soon as possible.

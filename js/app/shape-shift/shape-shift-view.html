ShapeShift is a third-party service offered by ShapeShift AG, a company based in Zug, Switzerland.

ShapeShift lets you convert (or "Shift") between some of the digital assets supported by Jaxx. Enter the amount you want to convert (either in currency or the digital asset currently being displayed), then, using the drop-down menu just below the "Shift" button, select what coin type you would like to convert to.

At the bottom of the ShapeShift display, you'll see the current conversion rate and the minimum and maximum transaction size allowed by ShapeShift.

Once you've made your selection, select Shift. This will bring you to a confirmation screen where you can review and complete the transaction.

Please note that ShapeShift transactions may take up to 10-20 minutes to appear in the target coin's wallet screen.

ShapeShift services are integrated into Jaxx as a convenience for you, at no-cost. All support inquiries should be directed to ShapeShift through their support page.

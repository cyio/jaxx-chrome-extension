"Spendable Balance" is the MAX amount that you can send out of your wallet. Your spendable balance is less than your total balance by the mining fee necessary to execute your transaction. Go to your settings menu to adjust the mining fee you're willing to offer bitcoin miners. Reducing the fee amount you offer may cause significant delays in your transactions. Note that bitcoin mining fees are determined by file size and not by the amount of BTC you're sending. The number of inputs and outputs in your wallet contributes to the file size.

Note also that bitcoin mining fees have risen in recent months due to extreme bitcoin network congestion.
